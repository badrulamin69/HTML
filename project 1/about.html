Amanullah University Collage

Amanullah University College is situated at Bhandaria in Pirojpur district. The illustrious industrialist, businessman, educationist and social reformer M.A Khalaque established this University College with a view to turning the educationally backward mass into human potential resources. Apart from having higher secondary level, it has four years honours course and degree pass course for higher education. Besides, to make the students creative and intellectual, there is also girl’s guide and rover scout. To make the future generation skillful, there is a gymnastic subject with co- curricular activities such as games and sports, debate competition, cultural ceremony, excursion etc. For the progress of poor and meritorious students, there is a consecutive stipend. Besides, there is continuation of special stipend by MAK Foundation for those students who get chance of higher education in BUET, Medical college and Dhaka University after completing Higher Secondary level from this College. To facilitate education, it has its own transport and distinct residential halls. The expert and experienced teachers are engaged in their respective activities under exact leading of the reverent principal for enlightening the poverty stricken mass of the southern part of Bangladesh. Above all, the campus which is enriched with modern amenities, is proceeding ahead with the oath of “ Education, Peace and Progress”.


Key Highlights:
Location: Bhandaria, Pirojpur District, Bangladesh.
Founder: M.A. Khalaque, a noted industrialist and educationist.
Programs: Offers Higher Secondary,
Degree Pass Course, and Four-Year Honours courses.
Focus: Promotes standard education, science-based knowledge, innovation, and ethical values.
Facilities: Provides classrooms, labs, library, dormitories, and sports facilities.
Co-curriculars: Encourages activities like sports, debates, cultural programs, and excursions.
Mission: To educate, inspire, empower, and foster leadership within the community.
